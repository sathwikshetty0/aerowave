Transforming Lives Through Technology

Addressing critical mobility challenges for India's hearing-impaired community through innovative AI technology

🎯

The Challenge

63 million hearing-impaired Indians face significant safety risks while riding, unable to detect vehicle horns, emergency sirens, and collision warnings. This limits their mobility, employment opportunities, and independence.

63M people affected nationwide
⚡

Our Solution

AeroWave uses advanced AI algorithms to detect, classify, and instantly convert environmental sounds into directional haptic feedback, giving deaf riders spatial awareness through touch.

95% detection accuracy achieved
🌟

The Impact

Legal compliance backed by Supreme Court judgment, RTO support, and life-changing employment opportunities in delivery, logistics, and transportation sectors across India.

∞ possibilities unlocked
